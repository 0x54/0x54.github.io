About

[0x01] Who am I?


My name is Francesco Matarazzo and 0x54.space is my personal blog. I earned a B.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Salerno, Italy. I’m a *Nix aficionado, a Pythonista and a free software advocate. I’m heavily interested in information security, distribuited systems as well as software engineering. I like both theorical and practical side. I love to build and break things, thinking “out of the box” and solving problems. I use to take advantage of information and computing power to build automatization. In the last 10 years I worked as software engineering, penetration tester, security consultant and *Nix system administrator. I’m also interested in new media arts, especially in the interactive part.
